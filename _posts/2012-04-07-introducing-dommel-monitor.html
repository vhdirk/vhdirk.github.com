After a bit of fooling around with Android, I finally got around in finishing my very first app.

Dommel Monitor is a dead simple isp monitor for customers of dommel.com. Since dommel does not provide some sort of API for their usage data, this app just logs in on the users dashboard, and parses some webpages to get to the actual data.
